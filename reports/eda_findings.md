\# EDA Findings



\## Mutual Fund Analytics – Day 3



\### Finding 1



NAV values across most schemes show a long-term upward trend between 2022 and 2025.



\### Finding 2



SBI Mutual Fund maintains one of the highest AUM levels among all fund houses.



\### Finding 3



Monthly SIP inflows have increased significantly over the analysis period, indicating growing retail participation.



\### Finding 4



Large Cap, Flexi Cap, Mid Cap, and Small Cap categories consistently attract strong inflows.



\### Finding 5



Total folio count increased substantially from 2022 to 2025, showing industry growth.



\### Finding 6



T30 cities contribute a larger share of transactions compared to B30 cities.



\### Finding 7



SIP transactions are more frequent than Lumpsum and Redemption transactions.



\### Finding 8



Most investors have completed KYC verification.



\### Finding 9



Expense ratios are concentrated within acceptable industry ranges.



\### Finding 10



Banking, Financial Services, IT, and related sectors occupy a significant portion of mutual fund portfolios.
